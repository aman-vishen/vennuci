01Blake Stitch
Material Science / Footwear & Accessories
Designed With Perfection
Blake stitch footwear is a construction method that uses single stitching for a lightweight, flexible and close-to-foot fit. It's also known for its sleek look, fast production and suitability for leather soles. It also provides more comfort by having the stitching done inside the shoe.
